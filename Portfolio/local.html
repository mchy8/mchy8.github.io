Downtown Home and Garden


A visit to the University of Michigan Matthaei Botanical Gardens is guaranteed to brighten your succulent-less day! Filled to the brim with strange fruits and interesting foliage- the botanical gardens has plants for all ages. Not only will you find interesting succulents and cacti, but also a special succulent kaleidoscope! If you get attached to the succulents, there is a giftshop with plants and all your gardening needs. Visit and support your local botanical gardens today. Free Admission!


University of Michigan


Downtown Home and Garden boasts a year-round greenhouse filled with different types of succulent, cacti and Venus fly traps! Even if you aren’t sure what type of succulent is right for you, perusing the ample stock of Downtown Home and Garden can help you chose the perfect plant for you! In addition, they have “Master Gardeners” on staff to aid you in your search. Further, there are weekly events that occur in their store like Science Sundays and gardening workshops.
